Please note for all Terminal Gate Prices:

  1. All prices are in Australian Cents and are inclusive of GST
  2. All prices quoted are at L15
  3. All prices are for:
    • spot purchases and do not apply to purchasers covered by a current supply contract with Liberty
    • customer pickup at the applicable terminal, in tanker loads of not less than 35,000 litres
  4. Payment in full (funds cleared), is to be made before pickup. This can be any form of approved prepayment or Telegraphic Transfer (TT). Any other payment arrangement will be by negotiation.
  5. All vehicles and drivers entering terminals must be accredited to meet the controlling company’s operational and safety requirements.
  6. All products are subject to availability.
  7. Prices include Capital City Petroleum Products subsidies where applicable.
  8. For new customers, it could take up to 10 working days to set up your account and enable your first pick up, and the price applicable will be the published TGP applicable for that day.
